About Us


Why Makoko Fresh? Makoko Fresh is a website created by SMAT team for fishermen in Makoko community in order to enable them sell their products to people outside Makoko community. We realised that there are lot of middle men between the fishermen and the final consumers. The fishermen who are the real husstlers do not make much gain like the middlemen so we decided to help them by building this platform so it will be between and the final consumers.
