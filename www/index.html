Who we are

Makoko Fresh is a website created by SMAT team for fishermen in Makoko community in order to enable them sell their products to people outside Makoko community.

What we sell

Our stock vary from season to season, you can check out our store to have a general idea of what we sell. Our order form updates with the current stock our fishermen have.

Why use us

We realised that there are lot of middle men between the fishermen and the final consumers. The fishermen who are the real husstlers do not make much gain like the middlemen so we decided to help them by building this platform so it will be between and the final consumers.
